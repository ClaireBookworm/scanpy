scvi-toolsÔÉÅ

  • scvi-tools Berkeley

    scvi-tools hosts deep generative models (DGM) for end-to-end analysis of single-cell omics data (e.g., scVI, scANVI, totalVI). It also contains several primitives to build novel DGMs.
